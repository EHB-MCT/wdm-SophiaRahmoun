# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making this educational project a welcoming and respectful environment for all participants, regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and considerate in all interactions
- Focus on what is best for the educational community
- Show empathy towards other learners and contributors
- Be collaborative and open to different perspectives
- Use inclusive language and avoid assumptions

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or derogatory comments
- Disrespectful or unprofessional conduct
- Misuse of the face analysis system for malicious purposes
- Attempts to use this educational prototype for real-world decision-making
- Sharing of private user data or analysis results

## Educational Usage

This project is designed exclusively for academic exploration of algorithmic bias. Users must:

- Understand that all analysis results are simulated and non-scientific
- Not attempt to apply findings to real individuals or groups
- Maintain awareness of the project's critical stance on biometric systems
- Use the system only to demonstrate bias and ethical concerns

## Enforcement

Project maintainers reserve the right to remove or block any participant who violates these standards, with priority given to maintaining a safe and productive educational environment.
